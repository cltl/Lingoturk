The task

In this task, you will be presented with:

  1. a full SENTENCE written by JOHN, optionally quoting some other PEOPLE/SOURCES
  2. an independent STATEMENT

Please indicate whether JOHN would AGREE or DISAGREE with the statement.
If there are other PEOPLE/SOURCES quoted in the sentence, please indicate their opinion with respect to the statement as well.

The labels

AGREEMENT If John beliefs what is in the sentence, he must believe that the statement is true.
DISAGREEMENT If John beliefs what is in the sentence, he must believe that the statement is false.
PARTIAL AGREEMENT If John beliefs what is in the sentence, he must believe that the statement is only partially true.
I CANNOT TELL The statement is about something else than the sentence, or it cannot be determined what John believes to be true.

Important

Base your question only on what is written in the text. Try not to guess based on what you know about pro- and anti-vaccine beliefs. For example:

  1. I am convinced that vaccines are 100% safe.

From this sentence, we can infer that John would disagree with a statement like "vaccines cause dangerous side effects".
After all, the statements "vaccines are 100% safe" and "vaccines cause dangerous side effects" cannot be true at the same time.

However, even though John is likely pro-vaccination, we CANNOT infer that John agrees with a statement like "vaccines are effective".
After all, the safety and effectiveness of vaccines are two different topics, so John could believe that vaccines are safe but ineffective at the same time.
